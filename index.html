ABOUT US

SR-DTU is a professional association dedicated to advancing technological innovation and excellence for the benefit of humanity. Its members inspire a global community through its highly cited conferences, technology standards, and professional and educational activities. With a very dedicated team we dare to touch a zenith beyond which robotics could not be defined any better. We, at SRDTU, focus equally on the practical knowledge as well as theoretical knowledge. We aim to bring out the best in our members by preparing them for various competitions held around the world as well as for the ones organized in our college. Our planning and execution team has already started its work to mould students to raise the benchmark of their success in the workshop and competitions.
